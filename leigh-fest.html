Leigh Festival

For those of you that don't know what Leigh Festival is, for a couple of years a friend and I wanted to create an inclusive festival for the people of Leigh and the surrounding area. Last year we hosted our first festival which included a huge bar with 36 different beers, lagers, ciders as well as a cocktail bar! Throughout the festival we had live music from local artists, mixed ability rugby, a sevens rugby tournament and much more.
All money raised is given to Leigh RUFC to help them continue running and provide the chance for people from aged 5 - 40+ to participate in Rugby Union.
