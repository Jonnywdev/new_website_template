Hi I'm Jonny!

My story

I graduated Graphic Design in 2020 with a 2:1. When I graduated, Covid was at its worst, no new jobs were available as all offices had closed. This was a big setback for me because I was set to begin an internship in graphic design in June 2020.

It was obvious to me after a couple of weeks of sitting around doing nothing that I had to get off my ass and find a job. As a result, I decided to apply for a job at Morrisons in Leigh. I worked there until September 2022. Working in a wide range of jobs, including Checkout Assistant, Click and Collect, Delivery Driver, etc.

The idea of learning a new skill in my free time was a desire of mine while I worked at Morrisons. I decided I wanted to bring one of my XD websites to life. At the time I was in the process of creating this site, I did not even know what HTML and CSS were, let alone how they worked. With the help of resources such as YouTube and Stackoverflow, I began to self-teach myself.

I took on the role of Communications Director at my local rugby club Leigh RUFC (Jan 2020). This gave me the opportunity to take on my first official project. Through COVID, we encountered a problem. It was going to be possible for us to open in 3 months, however only table service would be permitted. With the low number of staff we have, this would not have been feasible. Together, my friend (who had studied back-end development previously) and I created a successful table service website!

Feel free to contact me or check out my CV if you'd like to know more!
